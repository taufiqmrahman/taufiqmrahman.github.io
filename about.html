About Me

I am a passionate Computer Science & Engineering student at Brac University. With a deep love for problem-solving, software development, and everything tech, I constantly strive to build innovative solutions and sleek applications.

My technical journey has led me to explore low-level systems, full-stack web development, and object-oriented architectures. I believe good code is like good prose — clear, intentional, and easy to follow.

When I'm not writing code or debugging late into the night, you can find me exploring my hobbies: capturing life through photography, diving into cinematic worlds, following high-speed racing, getting lost in books, and gaming.

I believe in continuous learning, pushing boundaries, and always striving to craft something remarkable.
